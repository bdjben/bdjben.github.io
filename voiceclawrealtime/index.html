VoiceClaw Realtime

Realtime AI voice for iPhone, Apple Watch, and your own OpenClaw computer.

VoiceClaw Realtime lets you talk with GPT-Realtime-2 and GPT-5.5 Instant, optionally route requests to OpenClaw on your Mac, and use VoiceClaw Companion to pair your devices with your own OpenAI, OpenClaw, Tailscale, or HTTPS tunnel configuration.

Download Companion Setup and support

Live voice

GPT-Realtime-2 conversations

Start a user-controlled live voice session, speak naturally, interrupt responses, mute the microphone, and keep the conversation available while the active session remains open.

OpenClaw

Your computer, your setup

When paired with VoiceClaw Companion, VoiceClaw can reach OpenClaw on your Mac through Tailscale or an optional HTTPS tunnel that you configure.

Apple Watch

Voice and text on watchOS

Use Apple Watch to talk to GPT-Realtime-2, send text prompts, relay requests through iPhone, or reach an optional public HTTPS bridge when your iPhone is not nearby.

Accounts

Use your own credentials

VoiceClaw is designed for user-owned OpenAI credentials, OpenClaw OAuth through the Mac Companion, and user-controlled network routes rather than shared developer credentials.

Subscription

Pricing

VoiceClaw Realtime offers auto-renewable subscriptions through the App Store: monthly access for $14.99 per month and annual access for $59.99 per year, each with a 3-day free trial for eligible users. Prices may vary by region according to App Store pricing.

Billing, renewal, cancellation, and refunds are handled by Apple through the App Store. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
