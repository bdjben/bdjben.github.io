Installation readiness

For Codex / OpenClaw / Hermes installation sessions.

Dedicated Mac

Use a dedicated Mac, not a personal or primary work machine. Keep personal accounts and identifying device names off the setup. Optionally, your systems can be built on virtual machines or remote physical machines, accessible on your current hardware via remote desktop software. If desired, you can still access the solutions implemented for you on your existing personal or work devices without remote desktop software.

Local models

For the most sensitive matters, systems can be designed to exclusively use local AI models. Subject to availability of hardware capable of running the latest and most capable open-source models, with benchmark performance equal to current frontier models from the most popular AI providers.

Remote access

Implementation work can be expedited via remote access to your dedicated hardware, if preferred over in-person, on-site work.

Availability

Be physically present during the first hour of initial setup and reachable by phone for the remainder of the session. After the first session, we schedule progress check-ins, remote troubleshooting, and post-implementation follow-ups at times that are convenient for you.
